Real yield = nominal − inflation; Fisher = (1+nominal)/(1+inflation) − 1. Yields are OECD long-term (10Y) government bond rates; EUR uses the euro-area aggregate (EA20). The period next to each figure is the month/quarter of the latest available data — CPI releases lag by one to two months, and some series lag further in the OECD dataset. The page re-fetches the data every time it is opened or refreshed, and once an hour while it stays open, so it always shows the most recent published figures.
